Travel Essentials

Travel Essentials to pack :

    Documents and Money -

  • Passport / Visa / ID proof
  • Travel tickets (flight, train, bus, etc.)
  • Hotel booking confirmations
  • Travel insurance documents
  • Debit/Credit cards & some local currency
  • Emergency contact list
    Clothing and Accessories -

  • Comfortable clothes (weather-appropriate)
  • Sleepwear & innerwear
  • Light jacket / sweater
  • Swimwear (if needed)
  • Sunglasses, hat, or cap
  • Scarf or shawl
  • Comfortable walking shoes / slippers


Travel Glossary
Itinerary -
A planned schedule of a trip, including destinations, transport, and activities.
Backpacking -
A low-budget, independent travel style where all belongings are carried in a backpack.
Layover -
A short stop at an airport between connecting flights before reaching the final destination.
